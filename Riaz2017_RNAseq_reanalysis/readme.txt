This is the reanalysis of the Riaz et al dataset of RNASeq data from patients undergoing anti-PD-1 blockade. Raw data can be downloaded https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/geo/query/acc.cgi?acc=GSE91061
This analysis was performed for the manuscript Kirchhammer et al STM2022 by Marcel P. Trefny in 2020/21/22
